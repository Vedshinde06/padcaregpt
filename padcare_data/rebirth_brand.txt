Rebirth is PadCare’s sustainable stationery and lifestyle brand, built on the concept of circular design. Rebirth products are made using recycled sanitary waste processed through PadCare’s technology.

The brand focuses on conscious consumption and sustainable gifting for businesses and individuals.

Key Products

Recycled A4 copier paper

A5 notebooks and notepads

Affirmation card decks

Desk calendars

Plant pots made from recycled pads and plastic

Eco-friendly trophies

Custom corporate eco-gifts

Rebirth demonstrates that sanitary waste can be transformed into functional, aesthetically pleasing products while reinforcing sustainability and environmental responsibility.
